How To Play

Draw clues from cards, each card has a category of the clue and a range assigned to it.

Your deck value will increase by a random amount in the range of the category.

The game ends if you go over 21 on your deck value.

To Win: Guess the mystery player correctly.

To Lose: Bust or guess wrong.
